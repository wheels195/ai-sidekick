AI Sidekick Onboarding Playbook

Complete guide from first inquiry to fully operational system. Includes GoHighLevel setup, scripts, objections, and unit economics.

1
Inquiry & Discovery

How Leads Come In

  • Website form (ai-sidekick.io → "Get Your Free Consultation") — Collects name, phone, email, business type, biggest challenge
  • Vertical landing pages (hvac.html, dental.html, etc.)
  • Direct calls/texts from ads or referrals

When Someone Inquires

  1. Form shows "We got your info! We'll reach out within 24 hours."
  2. You receive the lead — name, phone, email, industry, challenge
  3. Personal outreach within 24 hours — call or text to schedule discovery call

Discovery Call Script (15-20 min)

Goal: Understand their business, pain points, and current process.

Questions to Ask

  • How many calls do you get per day/week?
  • What happens when you miss a call right now? (Voicemail? Nothing?)
  • Do you have someone answering phones, or is it just you?
  • How do you follow up with leads who got a quote but didn't book?
  • How many Google reviews do you have? Are you actively asking for them?
  • What's your average job/client worth? (Helps frame ROI)
  • What software do you currently use? (Scheduling, CRM, etc.)
  • What's your biggest frustration with how leads are handled today?

Key Stats to Cite

  • 85% of missed callers never call back (Smith.ai)
  • 78% of customers buy from the first business that responds
  • 21x more likely to convert when you respond within 5 minutes (MIT/InsideSales)
  • Receptionist costs $2,500-4,000/mo for 8hrs/day vs. $399/mo for 24/7/365

Kickoff & Information Gathering

Kickoff Call Agenda (30-45 min)

Business Information Needed

  • Business name, address, website
  • Business phone number (the one that triggers missed call text-back)
  • Business hours (when AI handles calls vs. live answering)
  • Services offered and typical pricing ranges
  • Service area / locations served
  • How they want appointments scheduled (time slots, duration, buffer time)

Brand Voice & Messaging

  • How do they talk to customers? (Casual? Professional?)
  • Any specific phrases they use or want included?
  • Anything the AI should NEVER say? (specific pricing, guarantees, etc.)
  • How should the AI introduce itself?
  • What info should the AI collect from leads? (Name, address, photos?)

Technical Setup Info

  • Current phone system/carrier
  • Existing scheduling software (if any)
  • Google Business Profile login (for review automation)
  • Any existing CRM data to migrate?

Review Automation

  • Link to their Google Business Profile
  • When should review requests go out?
  • Custom review request message preferences

System Build in GoHighLevel (24-48 hrs)
The customer never sees or touches GoHighLevel. Everything below is your internal setup.
Snapshot tip: If you've built a working setup for one industry, save it as a GHL Snapshot. Snapshots clone workflows, calendars, pipelines, and settings — apply to new sub-accounts and customize instead of building from scratch.

1. Sub-Account & Phone Setup

  • Create GHL sub-account for the customer
  • Fill in business info: name, address, timezone, language
  • Purchase or port business phone number via LC Phone (~$1.15/mo local, ~$2.15/mo toll-free)
  • Complete A2P 10DLC registration (mandatory for US SMS) — brand registration $4-12 one-time, campaign $4/mo, takes 1-5 business days
  • Configure call forwarding / missed call trigger
  • Set up business hours vs. after-hours rules

2. Missed Call Text-Back

Basic method: Settings → "Enable Missed Call Text Back" → customize message

Workflow method (recommended):

  • Automations → Workflows → Create Workflow
  • Trigger: "Inbound Call" with Call Status = "Missed"
  • Enable "Run Once Per Contact"
  • Add wait step (10-30 sec delay)
  • Add "Send SMS" action with custom brand-voice message
  • Add "Push Notification to Assigned User" for owner alerts
  • Set up Conversation AI for two-way SMS responses
  • Test: call the number, don't answer, verify text fires
SMS Costs: Standard 10DLC: $0.0119/msg • Toll-free: $0.0175/msg • MMS: $0.03/msg • Messages over 160 chars = multiple segments

3. AI Employee Setup (Voice + Conversation AI)

$0.13/min for Voice AI, or $97/mo unlimited per sub-account.

  • Enable AI Employee in Agency Settings → Company tab
  • Configure Conversation AI for SMS/chat (greeting, tone, personality)
  • Train on services, pricing ranges, FAQs, booking rules
  • Set up intent detection (booking, quote, emergency, general)
  • Configure Voice AI Agent (optional) for live call answering
  • Set boundaries: topics AI should never discuss

4. Appointment Booking

  • Calendars → Calendar Settings → Create Service Calendar
  • Configure appointment types, durations, buffer time
  • Set availability windows matching business schedule
  • Link to owner's Google Calendar (prevent double-booking)
  • Set up automated confirmation SMS/email
  • Build reminder workflow: 24hr + 1hr before
  • Enable payment collection if needed (Stripe, Square)
  • Create booking link for AI to share in conversations

5. Lead Follow-Up Sequences

  • Set up pipeline: New → Contacted → Quoted → Booked → Completed
  • Build quote follow-up: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
  • Build new lead nurture sequence
  • Configure human handoff triggers
  • Set up "Goal" actions — stop sequence when contact books or replies

6. Review Automation

  • Connect Google Business Profile
  • Use Guided Review Setup Wizard
  • Build post-job review request workflow (trigger on "Completed" stage, 2hr delay)
  • Send SMS with direct Google review link
  • One follow-up reminder if no review in 3 days
  • Enable Reviews AI (Suggestive mode initially)
  • Set up "Review Received" trigger — auto-respond to 5-star, alert owner for 1-3 star

7. Dashboard & Reporting

  • Configure owner notifications (real-time SMS/push for new leads)
  • Set up monthly report template (calls caught, conversations, bookings, follow-ups, reviews)
  • Add owner as Staff user only if they want limited dashboard access
5
Testing & Launch

Internal Testing (Before Going Live)

  • Call the business number, let it ring — verify text-back fires within seconds
  • Reply to the text — verify AI responds naturally in brand voice
  • Book an appointment through the AI — verify it shows on calendar
  • Test appointment reminders (confirmation + reminder sequence)
  • Trigger a follow-up sequence — verify timing and messaging
  • Send a test review request — verify link goes to correct Google profile
  • Test after-hours vs. business hours behavior
  • Test human handoff scenario

Launch Call with Customer (15-20 min)

  1. Live demo — "I'm going to call your number right now. Watch your phone." Show the text-back, AI conversation, and booked appointment.
  2. Day-to-day expectations — Notifications for new leads, appointments on calendar, automatic follow-up, they can take over any conversation manually.
  3. Set expectations — First recovered lead within days. Monthly report at end of month 1. "Text/call me to adjust anything."
  4. Confirm notification preferences — Real-time alerts or daily summary?

Monthly Ongoing

  • Send monthly performance report

  • Adjust follow-up timing/messaging
  • Monitor review generation
  • Quarterly strategy call (optional, builds retention)

Handling Objections

"$1,500 setup is expensive."
"That covers building a fully custom system trained on your business — your voice, your services, your hours. It's not a template. And it pays for itself the first time it catches a $2,000+ job from a missed call."
"$399/month is a lot."
"A part-time receptionist costs $1,500-2,000/month and only works during business hours. This works 24/7/365, handles unlimited conversations, follows up automatically, and gets you reviews. If it recovers even one extra job per month, it more than pays for itself."
"I don't get that many missed calls."
"Most business owners are surprised. We typically find 20-40 missed calls per month that went straight to voicemail. Even if it's just 10, at your average job value, that's significant revenue walking away."
"I tried something like this before and it didn't work."
"Most tools give you software and expect you to figure it out. We do everything for you — setup, customization, optimization. You don't log into anything. And there's no contract, so if it's not working, you can cancel anytime."
"Can I see it in action first?"
"Absolutely. Give me your cell number — I'll set up a quick demo right now and you can see exactly what your customers would experience."
"I need to think about it."
"Totally understand. While you're thinking, keep track of how many calls go to voicemail this week. That's the revenue gap we close. I'll follow up [specific day]."
