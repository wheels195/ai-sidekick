How it works

Five systems. One setup.

We configure everything for your business in 48 hours. Here's exactly what each system does, how we set it up, and what we need from you.

01

AI Voice Receptionist

Your AI Sidekick answers your business phone 24/7. It greets callers, answers questions about your services, pricing, and hours, books appointments on your calendar, and transfers calls to you when needed.

  • Answers calls during business hours, after hours, and weekends
  • Configured with your actual services, pricing, and FAQs
  • Books appointments directly on your calendar
  • Transfers to you or your team for urgent calls
  • Every call is recorded — review from your dashboard or mobile app
  • English and Spanish language support
  • 1We purchase a local phone number for your business
  • 2We connect it to your existing business number — you choose whether AI Sidekick picks up unanswered calls only, or handles all calls during specific hours
  • 3We configure your AI Sidekick with your services, pricing, hours, and common customer questions
  • 4You review and approve the responses before going live
Setup time: 30-60 minutes

02

Missed Call Text-Back

When a call goes unanswered, the system automatically texts the caller within seconds. The text acknowledges their call and starts a conversation over SMS, so you can capture the lead even when you can't pick up.

  • Texts go out within seconds of a missed call
  • Customized message with your business name
  • Conversation continues over text to book the appointment
  • Works around the clock, including nights and weekends
  • Jump into any conversation from your phone — AI Sidekick steps back automatically

04

Automated Lead Follow-Up

When a lead doesn't book right away, the system sends a series of automated text messages over the following days. Each message is timed and written to re-engage the lead and get them to schedule.

  • Multi-step text sequences over days or weeks
  • Stops automatically when the lead books or replies
  • Customized for your business (quotes, estimates, service follow-ups)
  • All conversations visible in your dashboard and mobile app
  • Leads can reply STOP anytime to opt out — fully automatic
  • 1We build your follow-up sequences based on your typical customer journey (quote sent, estimate given, etc.)
  • 2Your AI Sidekick generates follow-up messages configured for your industry and services, sent on a timed schedule
  • 3Sequences trigger automatically when a new lead comes in and doesn't book

05

Google Review Automation

After a job is completed, the system automatically sends your customer a text message with a direct link to leave you a Google review. More reviews means more visibility and more trust with new customers.

  • Sends a direct Google review link via text
  • Triggers automatically after job completion
  • One tap for the customer — opens Google directly
  • Track how many requests are sent in your dashboard
  • 1We look up your business on Google and set up the review request automation
  • 2After a completed job, the system texts your customer asking them to leave a review
  • 3One tap takes the customer straight to your Google review page
