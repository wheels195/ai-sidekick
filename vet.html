How it works

Set it up once. It handles the rest.

01
We configure your clinic's system
We connect to your clinic's phone line and configure your AI with your services, appointment types, office hours, and emergency protocols. Setup takes less than 48 hours with zero disruption to your practice.
02
Every missed call gets a text instantly
When a call goes unanswered, the pet owner receives a friendly text within seconds. Your AI Sidekick asks about their pet, the reason for the visit, and books the right appointment type — wellness, sick visit, surgery consult, or refill request.

Every new client inquiry gets an automated follow-up sequence. After appointments, pet owners are prompted to leave a Google review. Vaccine and checkup reminders go out automatically to keep clients bonded to your practice.
