Common Questions

Do I need to learn any software?
Not at all. We handle everything. You'll get notifications when leads come in and see appointments on your calendar, but the system runs on autopilot. If you can send a text, you're good.
Will customers know they're talking to AI?
No. We customize every message to match your voice and tone. Customers typically can't tell the difference — responses feel natural, helpful, and personal.
What if someone needs to talk to a real person?
AI Sidekick knows when to step aside. Complex situations get routed directly to you or your team instantly. You can also jump into any conversation at any time.
What happens after hours?
Instead of going to voicemail (where most people hang up), callers get a friendly text within seconds. AI Sidekick keeps the conversation going and many leads turn into booked appointments — even at 11pm on a Saturday.
When will I start seeing results?
Most businesses see their first recovered lead within the first few days. By the end of month one, you'll have clear data on calls caught, leads followed up, and reviews collected.
What if I want to cancel?
No contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime with a simple message. We believe the results speak for themselves.
